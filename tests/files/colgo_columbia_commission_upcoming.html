Commission Meetings

All Are Welcome

Come to our meetings to participate and hear what's happening! 
 
The Gorge Commission welcomes everyone to attend Commission meetings. The Commission typically holds its regular monthly meetings on the second Tuesday of each month. Special meetings are also scheduled as needed, and various committees meet several times a year. Below is the agenda for the next regularly scheduled meeting, upcoming special Commission meetings, and committee meetings. Most attachments referenced in the written minutes are not typically attached to the online minutes but are available upon request at the Commission office.
